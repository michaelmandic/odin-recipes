Peanut Butter Bars

Description

Delicious and easy-to-make peanut butter bars with a rich, creamy texture and a sweet, nutty flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together the peanut butter, melted butter, powdered sugar, and graham cracker crumbs until well combined.
  2. Press the mixture evenly into the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the chocolate chips and vegetable oil. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring after each, until the chocolate is melted and smooth.
  4. Pour the melted chocolate over the peanut butter layer and spread it evenly with a spatula.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until the chocolate is set.
  6. Cut into bars and serve.
